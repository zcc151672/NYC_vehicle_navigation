NYC Traffic Accident Analysis Based on Motor Vehicle Collision (MVC) Data

Introduction


Worldwide, motor vehicle collisions lead to excessive impact to both individuals and society. As for individuals, injuries, disability, long-lasting psychological trauma, and even death may unfortunately occur from these issues. As for society, financial costs can occur to constrain the development of cities. Residents may feel the sense of insecurity as well.

Residents and citizens expect to see the decreasing and reduction of the occurrence of motor vehicle collision. Assuming we are the staff in government office of New York City, our obligation is the statistics and analysis of New York City MVC data. The results and conclusions may be sent to New York Traffic Bureau or other agencies to assist them take actions.
